--- title: Helping Us To Help Others ---

The Rural North West Peterborough Good Neighbours Scheme

Our charity exists to give neighbourly support to older and less mobile and isolated people in parishes of Bainton and Ashton, Castor and Ailsworth and Glinton.

At the core of the charity's operations is a large team of local volunteers who help to deliver a variety of services to support vulnerable people in their communities.

We provide, befriending, advice and support, transport, help with shopping, light gardening and much more besides.

Any donation you are able to make will help us continue to provide this valuable community service
